Fantasy Football Chain

How it works

Step 1. Create a squad

Create a virtual team of football players that you think will receive performance points for scored goals, assists or clean sheats in the next round of the selected league.

Step 2. Stake some money

Fund your squad with ether cryptocurrency that you want to put at stake.

Step 3. Double your stake

If your team manages to outperform 50% of other teams then you double your stake. You will get back your doubled stake from Fantasy Football Chain smart contract which resides in Ethereum network.
